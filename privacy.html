Privacy Policy

Last updated 13 July 2026

Napkin Proofs is a math-education channel operated by Inceptyon Labs LLC. The "Napkin Proofs Publisher" application is a private, internal tool used solely to publish the channel's own videos to the channel's own social media accounts. It is not offered to, or usable by, the public.

What we collect

The application does not collect, request, or store personal data from any third party or viewer. It does not run analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling of any kind.

Account access

The application uses official platform APIs (including TikTok, YouTube, and Meta) with access granted by the account owner through OAuth. That access is used for one purpose only: to upload the channel's own video content to the channel's own accounts. Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and are never shared with any third party.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data. The only data transmitted is the channel's own video files and their captions, sent to the respective platform for the purpose of publishing them.

Data retention

Locally stored authorization tokens persist only as long as needed to operate the publishing tool and can be revoked at any time by the account owner through the platform's own settings.
